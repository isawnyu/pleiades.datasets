    Roman/late antique settlement, with visible remains of streets and mud-brick structures, including a church, two pigeon towers, and possibly residential units. Largely unexcavated.

    Contributors: Sean Gillies, Tom Elliott, Tom Elliott

    Actors: Nicola Aravecchia, Tom Elliott
  • Deir Abu Matta
    Late antique settlement, possibly monastic, with substantial remains of a church, mud-brick structures, and tombs.
    Creators: Nicola Aravecchia
    Contributors: Jeffrey Becker, Sean Gillies, Tom Elliott, Tom Elliott
    Modifications: precise coordinates, extensive description
    Actors: Tom Elliott
