Pleiades Change Log: 2011-04-01 - 2011-04-31

New Place Resources

  • Calavon (river)
    Tributary of the Druentia river (modern Durance). Its ancient name is unknown.
    Creators: Sean Gillies
    Contributors: Jeffrey Becker, Tom Elliott
  • Cyrenaica (region)
    An ancient region (the environs of the city of Cyrene) that lies in the eastern part of modern Libya.
    Creators: D.J. Mattingly
    Contributors: R. Talbert, Sean Gillies, Tom Elliott, Jeffrey Becker
  • Medinet Habu
    The New Kingdom mortuary temple of Ramesses III at Medinet Habu, on the West bank of the Nile near Luxor.
    Creators: Nate Nagy
    Contributors: Jeffrey Becker, Sean Gillies, Tom Elliott
  • Nilus (river)
    The Nile is a major river of northeastern Africa and is generally acknowledged as the longest river in the world, measuring some 6,650 km (4,130 miles) from source to mouth.
    Creators: A. Bernand
    Contributors: Brady Kiesling, R. Talbert, Jeffrey Becker, W. Röllig, Tom Elliott, H. Kopp, Sean Gillies, B. Siewert-Mayer, Francis Deblauwe, Eric Kansa
  • Trimithis
    An ancient settlement of at least Roman and Late Antique date, situated in the Dakleh Oasis at a site now called Amheida.
    Creators: G. Wagner, Tom Elliott
    Contributors: R. Talbert, Adam Prins, Jen Thum, Bruno Bazzani, Jeffrey Becker, DARMC, Herbert Verreth, Sean Gillies, Mark Depauw
